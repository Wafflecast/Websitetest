Our Mission

At Dis-Cleaner, we are committed to keeping Discord safe for everyone, especially protecting underaged users from harmful individuals. Our primary goal is to help servers identify and prevent predatory behavior, creating a cleaner, safer environment for users of all ages.

We understand the importance of maintaining privacy while also ensuring a secure platform, which is why our bot is designed to detect and remove inappropriate content and predatory actions. We believe that a safe environment fosters a better community and we strive to make Discord a place where everyone can feel safe.

We are always improving our bot’s capabilities and working closely with Discord to ensure we can protect all users from harm. Together, we can make the internet a safer place for everyone.
